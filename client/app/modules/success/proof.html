Proving Your Identity


To file a return, you need to prove your identity.


Your driver’s licence has all you need. But, if you don’t have a licence that’s valid for at least 2 weeks after you place your order, you’ll need your Tax Registration Number (TRN), proof of address, and a photo ID.


You can use any of these as proof of address:

  • Utility bill in your name (within the past 3 months)
  • Signed statement from a Justice of the Peace (JP)

You can use these as Photo ID:

  • Voter's ID
  • Passport
  • Passport-sized photos with a signed statement from a Justice of the Peace (JP)
