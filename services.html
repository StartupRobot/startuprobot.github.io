National Contracts Commission Registration (NCC)

From JM$20,000

Registering with the National Contracts Commission allows your business to apply for government contracts worth $500,000 or more.

To apply for an NCC Certificate (Goods and Services), you’ll need:

  • A TCC.
  • A Letter of Good Standing from the Companies Office.
  • Certified copies of Articles of Incorporation.
  • A profile of your company.
  • Qualifications and certifications of your project team.
  • 2 letters of recommendation.

NCCs are valid for 18 months. You will need to certify copies of all documents.
